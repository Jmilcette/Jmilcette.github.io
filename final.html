Final Project

Below is my final project for my CSC106 class. I spent the last month working with a partner to create a replica of the well known game "Asteroids". While we weren't able to replicate every feature, we enjoyed the experience of collaborating on a project and having a working final product.
The game is titled "Asteroids" as seen on the start screen. The premise of the game is very easy as described on the instructions page. You will use the forward arrow key to thrust forward, and the left and right arrow keys will be used to tilt the ship either left or right to change the direction of your thrust. You will be navigating through Asteroids that are coming from either side of the page. You can play the game Below!
